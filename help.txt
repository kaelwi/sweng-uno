The goal of the game is to reach 500 points.
The player which gets rid of his cards first in a round gets all the points from the remaining cards of the other players.

You are allowed to play a card if the card on the discard deck has the same color or the same value. Except for wild cards. These don't have any color, you may play them on any other color.

Special cards:
+2: The next player has to take 2 cards.
